What I do other than eating and sleeping...

Innovate. Impact. Inspire.

Buddy

  • Buddy is an emotion regulating assistant. It tracks emotions and regulates them by offering suggestions to college students to overcome anxiety and depression.
  • Addresses the anxiety issues, such as loneliness, stress and relationship issues, faced by over 80% of university students.
  • Developed at the Clinton Global Initiative Univeristy Codeathon in UC Berkeley in April 2016.

Actify

  • Actify provides online and offline professional development courses to help low-income individuals learn the skills they need for the job they want.
  • Closes the skills gap and allows local professionals to give back.
  • Selected as a regional Finalist in the Hult Prize Challenge, the largest social entrepreneurship competition, held in March 2016.
